Cat Paper Collection

As reported by Cisco, 90% of net traffic will be visual, and indeed, most of the visual data are cat photos and videos. Thus, understanding, modeling and synthesizing our feline friends becomes a more and more important research problem these days, especially for our cat lovers.

 

Cat Paper Collection is an academic paper collection that includes computer graphics, computer vision and machine learning papers that produce experimental results related to cats. If you would like to add/remove a paper, please send an email to Jun-Yan Zhu (junyanz at berkeley dot edu).
